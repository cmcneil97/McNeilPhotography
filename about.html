About Me

Ciaran is a highly experienced IT professional with a successful track record of working at multiple companies. At Tesco Bank in Edinburgh, he served as a Platform Insight Technical Engineer from 2018 to 2020. In this role, he was responsible for several key initiatives such as upgrading the internally hosted infrastructure of Splunk, implementing security patching for servers, and documenting the implementation of APM integration with ServiceNow. In 2020, Ciaran joined TSB Bank in Edinburgh and held multiple roles, including IT Operations Bridge Assistant Manager and Midrange Assistant Manager until March 2022. In these roles, he oversaw various critical functions, such as ensuring that processes, controls, and reporting were mapped in the strategic ITSM toolset, monitoring and managing tickets using various tools, owning the problem and incident records, managing the risk log, and deputizing for the Technical Operations Managers and other Technical Operations analysts as required. Overall, Ciaran has honed his skills in Splunk, Datadog, SQL, Linux, ServiceNow, AWS, Slack, ChatGPT, Jira, and Github. He has also earned several certifications, including the Splunk Certified User certification and the IT Diploma SCQF Level 8 - Computer Systems, Networking, and Telecommunications.
